Spencer Frazier Photography

These are some photographs of Spencer Frazier.

spencer frazier is a portrait and lifestyle image maker, he hails from the mile high city of Denver, Colorado.

he is a portrait and lifestyle image maker.

he also tells very painful dad jokes, and usually has to explain them afterward to get a laugh.

he takes photos in southern California, where he lives.

a Polaroid OneStep was his first camera.

he grew up shooting polaroids of his friends when he was young.

he went into college as an art criticism and gallery managment major at the University of Nebraska,

after he was finished eating corn and going to every home football game for

the Huskers, he moved to Santa Barbara, California and recieved a Bachlors in Fine Arts for Commercial Photography.
